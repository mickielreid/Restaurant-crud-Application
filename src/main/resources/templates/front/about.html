Our History

Maximizing life with a touch of luxury — many rave that LUXE MAIN is the best restaurant in Toronto. Under the culinary leadership of award-winning Executive Chef Lorenzo Loseto, LUXE MAIN has become a top-rated and best restaurant for the city of Toronto’s fine dining. Chef Loseto is widely regarded as one of the best restaurant chefs in the country today. LUXE MAIN Restaurant is the ultimate Toronto destination for an elevated food experience.

Our kitchen creative is in the hands of Executive Chef Michael Reid, an award-winning professional who has been inspiring taste buds for over a decade. His recent win as Canada’s Gold Plate Chef confirms his standing as one of the country’s greats. Known for creating globally inspired dishes, LUXE MAIN’s seasonally sourced menus promise intriguing and memorable dishes for the most discerning palates. Available as à la carte or tasting menus, LUXE MAIN also offers vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options.
